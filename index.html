Frequently Asked Questions

▶ What is Hourly Pay?

Hourly pay allows freelancers to get paid based on their working hours. Payments are processed securely on a weekly or bi-weekly basis.

▶ Can I take a second job while working full-time?

Yes, you can apply for part-time jobs while working full-time. Your job applications remain private unless you disclose them.

▶ How do projects work on Hitaishi Pay?

You can find freelance and contract-based projects. Payments can be milestone-based or completed upon project delivery.

▶ Do you offer training programs?

Yes, Hitaishi Pay provides skill training in various domains, including programming and digital marketing, with certification.

▶ How can I get technical support?

For any technical issues, contact our support team via email or live chat for quick assistance.

▶ Are there job opportunities for freshers?

Yes, freshers can apply for entry-level roles and internships. Enhance your profile with skills and training programs to boost your chances.
